Security and Risk Management - Summary Post (Unit 9)

Analyzing the relevance of CVSS

April 1, 2024 Wiley Finch

The CVSS is mostly criticized by Spring et al. (2021) for its misuse as a Risk Assessment tool. Analyzing the proposed literature stated by the article all three sources introduce the CVSS as a vulnerability assessment tool (Scarfone et al., 2008; Souppaya and Scarfone, 2013; PCI security standards, 2022). Most of the critical claims made by Spring et al. (2021) seem to originate from the interpretation of terminology between vulnerability and risk, which Wolf (2012) compared to the Babylonian confusion.

Therefore to rightfully analyze the article by Spring et al. (2021), it is important to define these terms. Stallings and Brown (2015) distinguish between vulnerabilities, threats, and risks. This article makes a correlation between these three and how they complement and evolve into eventually a risk. As claimed by the CVSS, the calculator should not be used for a risk score.
